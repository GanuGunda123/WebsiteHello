The Pillar of Her Home

Born under stars with a radiant light, She’s the eldest daughter, bold and bright.
A beacon of strength, a guiding flame, Bearing her family’s honored name.

Her shoulders bear the weight of dreams, A river of duty in endless streams.
She rises early, works till late, Her hands weave futures, her heart shapes fate.

In her eyes, the fire of a thousand suns, A quiet resolve, where courage runs.
Through storms she walks, her steps so steady, A tower of strength, always ready.

She guards the legacy, rich and vast, A bridge between future and the past.

Destination

Guided by a legend, Commodore Rabhi Ahuja, A Padma Shri laureate, a beacon in the sea's expanse.
At 89, his wisdom anchors our journey, And I, so young, stood humbled beneath his vast command.

The waves first called me in 2015, a novice, A new entry with dreams adrift in salty winds.
Now, years have passed, and with pride, I stand, The senior-most cadet, leading the ship’s grand dance.

Each visit fills my heart with boundless joy, The Sea Cadets' deck, my haven, my joy.

Her Hobby

Reading Books

In a quiet nook, where the sunlight streams, She journeys beyond the realm of dreams.
A book in her hands, her sanctuary found, Lost in the whispers where stories abound.

The rustle of pages, a comforting song, Each word a step where her thoughts belong.
Through valleys of wisdom, through skies of lore, She dances with tales, forever exploring more.

Her eyes trace lines of wonder and grace, Each sentence a map, each chapter a place.
From ancient worlds to futures untold, She uncovers treasures more precious than gold.

Books are her haven, her solace, her guide, In their embrace, she needs no tide.
For in their worlds, she's both the seeker and sage, An eternal voyager, a queen of the page.
